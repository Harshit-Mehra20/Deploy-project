DETECTION METHODS

How Our AI Engine Works

GAN Fingerprint Detection

Generative Adversarial Networks leave subtle statistical artifacts in the frequency domain. Our CNN model has been trained on 500K+ real and fake image pairs to identify these invisible fingerprints.

Frequency Analysis

Real photographs have characteristic noise patterns in their high-frequency components. Deepfakes show unnatural smoothness in these bands, revealing AI generation even when visually perfect.

Facial Geometry Mapping

We map 468 facial landmarks and check for anatomical inconsistencies — asymmetries, blending artifacts around hairlines, ears, and teeth that are telltale signs of face-swapping.

Metadata Forensics

Every genuine photo embeds EXIF data — camera model, GPS, timestamps. We cross-check this data for anomalies and signs of stripping or manipulation that indicate post-processing.
